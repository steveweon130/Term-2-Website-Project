HOW TO WIN IN PUBG?

START A GAME

When you enter the main menu, simply press START to start matchmaking. You can play PUBG as Solo, Duo, and as Squad. If you want, you can challenge yourself by playing 1-Man Squad where you will join a squad game alone. So, most fights you'll meet are going to be 1 vs 4.
You can play in 5 different maps: Erangel, Miramar, Sanhok, Vikendi, and Karakin.


Here are the key guides for the beginners since PUBG doesn't give a tutorial.

LAND ON A PLACE

In the beginning of this game, you are in a giant cargo plane flying over a huge map. At certain point, you are allowd to jump and parachute your way.
Usually good loots are more lkely to be spawned at a big places like cities. But more people would be landing there, so it isn't that easy to go to the big cities.

CONSUUMABLES

Consumables are a class of items that take time to use. Once used, the item is removed from the inventory and cannot be recovered or reused.
There are 2 types of consumables: Medical and Gear Consumables.

GETTING INTO SAFE ZONE

BLUE ZONE

The Blue Zone is a circular area that appears on the map. Within the confines of the Blue Zone players fight one another; existing outside the Blue Zone inflicts periodically increasing damage that will eventually kill players.
The radius of the Blue Zone decreases during the match, making the playable area of the map smaller and smaller.
Also, the damage in the Blue Zone increases as the Blue Zone decreases smaller and smaller.

RED ZONE

A red zone is a danger zone which occurs randomly on map after the game begins. After a certain period of time, the zone gets bombed and once hit, player receives great damage. If you're in a building within the red zone or out of the red zone, you can avoid the bombing
