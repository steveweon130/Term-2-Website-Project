WHAT IS PUBG?

PlayerUnknown's BattleGrounds (PUBG) is an online multiplayer First and Third Person Shooter (FPS/TPS) Battle Royale Game developed and published by PUBG Corporation, a subsidiary of South Korean video game company Bluehole on December 20, 2017.
The name "PlayerUnknown" is taken from the main desiger of PUBG, Brendan Greene whose game IDs are named as "PlayerUnknown".

Game Genre Origin

The game genre of PUBG is taken from a Japanese movie called "Batte Royale"(2000) where 42 students are put on an isolated island and kill each other out until one survives.
This is why those games with "last man standing" genres like PUBG are called "Batte Royale Games".
For PUBG, some of the clothes the characters from the movie are actually in the game.
For example, the school uniform of the female character in the PUBG trailer was from the movie.
If you want to watch this movie, go to Youtube and watch it. (But, I don't recommend it. ^^;;)

GAMING PLAFORMS

In PUBG, there are four plaforms you can play PUBG with:

1. WINDOWS OR PC

This version is the very first platfom of PUBG which Early Access Version was released on March 23, 2017 and fully released on December 20, 2017.
This version is the most famous version and you can buy this game from Steam for $29.99 US.
However, in order to run this game, your computer requires some system requirements like GPUs. So, this is why it is hard to play PUBG on Laptops and Macs.
So, the company later released PUBG Lite, a free version of PUBG that is meant to be better playable on low-end computers by having significant reductions in graphic details and other features on October 10, 2019.
You can know more about PUBG System Requirements in this Website.

2. XBOX ONE

This version is the second platfom of PUBG which was released on September 4, 2018.
You can buy this game as a package with XBox One or just the game it self for $24.99 US.
They were actually thinking abot cross-platform play style between the PC and XBox users, but it would be unfair since keyboard and mouse are better than joysticks in this case.

3. MOBILE DEVICES

This version is the third platfom of PUBG which was released on February 9, 2018. in China at first and was released worldwide on March 19, 2018 with English versions.
This version was mainly developed by Chinese Tencent's Timi Studio and both available for Android and iOS for FREE. This game is too was laggy to play if you had an old mobile device. So, the company released a version meant for lower-end mobile devices PUBG Mobile Lite on July 25, 2019.

4. PLAYSTATION 4

This version is the last platfom of PUBG which was released on December 7, 2018. You can buy this version for $29.99 US.
This version has platform-exclusive customization items that other versions do not have such as costumes from the other PS4 games.
Cross-platform play support between the Xbox and PlayStation versions of Battlegrounds was added in October 2019 following a testing period during the prior month.
